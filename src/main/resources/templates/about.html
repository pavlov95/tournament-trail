About TournamentTrail

Helping chess players compete beyond their home city.

TournamentTrail brings tournament discovery, registration, travel coordination, and organiser tools into one platform.

Discover Find upcoming chess tournaments.
Compete Register and track participation details.
Travel Coordinate journeys with other players.

The idea

Why TournamentTrail exists

The problem

Chess tournaments are often published across different websites, social media pages, and local communities. Players may need to search through multiple sources before finding the event details they need.

Travelling to another city can also be expensive and difficult to organise, especially when players do not know who else is attending from their area.

The solution

TournamentTrail provides one place where players can browse events, review participation requirements, register for tournaments, and track their status.

Players can also create or join travel groups connected to a specific tournament and coordinate the journey with other participants.

Different roles

One platform, several responsibilities

Players

Browse events, register, create travel groups, and manage personal activity.

Organisers

Publish tournaments, update details, and manage registrations.

Administrators

Review organiser applications, manage roles, and moderate content.

Our goal

Make tournament participation easier from discovery to arrival.

TournamentTrail reduces the organisational work around chess events, so players can focus more on preparation and competition.
