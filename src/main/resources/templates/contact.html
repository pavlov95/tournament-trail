FAQ

Using TournamentTrail

Do I need an account to browse tournaments?

No. Public tournaments can be viewed without an account. You need to create an account and log in before registering for a tournament, joining a travel group, or using private features.

How do I register for a tournament?

Open the tournament details page and select Register for tournament. You must be logged in and meet any requirements listed by the organiser. Your registration and payment status will then appear on your dashboard.

How do I join a travel group?

Open a tournament and view its available travel groups. Select a group, review its public details, and submit a join request. The group owner decides whether to approve or reject the request.

Who can create tournaments?

Only approved organisers and administrators can create tournaments. A regular player must first submit an organiser application and receive administrator approval.

How do I become an organiser?

Create an account, log in, and submit an organiser application. An administrator will review it. If approved, your account receives organiser permissions and you can create and manage tournaments.

Are travel-group comments and budgets private?

Yes. Private comments, member information, meeting details, budgets, and expenses should be visible only to accepted group members, the group owner, and authorised administrators when moderation is required.

Will the site help me if my travel group cheated me of money?

TournamentTrail can provide platform records and allow you to report users or suspicious activity, but it does not hold payments and cannot guarantee reimbursement. Avoid sending money without clear agreement and proof. For suspected fraud, contact your payment provider and, when appropriate, the relevant authorities.

How do I become a GM?

Grandmaster is an official chess title awarded by FIDE. TournamentTrail cannot grant it. It normally requires meeting FIDE rating and performance requirements in eligible rated events. Consult the current FIDE title regulations and your national chess federation for the exact process.

What do I need to bring if this is my first rated tournament?

Check the organiser's instructions before travelling. You may need identification, your federation or player number, proof of registration or payment, and any equipment specifically requested by the organiser. Arrive early and confirm the venue, starting time, time control, and tournament rules.

Can I cancel a tournament registration?

Cancellation depends on the tournament's rules and current status. If cancellation is enabled, use the option on the tournament or registration page. Refunds are controlled by the organiser's policy and are not automatically guaranteed by TournamentTrail.

How is my payment status confirmed?

Payment is made according to the organiser's instructions. The organiser reviews the payment information and updates your status. TournamentTrail displays that status but does not process the payment unless a payment service is added in the future.
